Our Mission

Inspired by Deuteronomy 14:29, we extend God’s hand to orphans, widows, and the STR. We believe that caring for the vulnerable is the heart of true faith.

Religion that God our Father accepts as pure and faultless is this: to look after widows and orphans in their distress.

Empowerment Workshops

Monthly gatherings for financial literacy, skill-building, and spiritual encouragement.

Community Outreach

Weekly meal distributions, prayer circles, and home visits for ongoing support.

Safe Housing

Temporary shelter for widows in crisis, with on-site counseling and care.

Legal & Financial Aid

Assistance navigating inheritance laws, benefits applications, and budgeting.
